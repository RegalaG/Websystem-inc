What is STAGES?

Life puts in front of us a primary
confusion that stops us in our tracks--

Who have I been?
Who am I now?
Who am I becoming?

These questions make us slow down
until we solve the developmental
confusions we are facing

How can we get feedback on
our healthy contradictions,
and discover the new version
of who we are?
STAGES Matrix is a tool that can guide us through this journey. It won't put us into a box that has ceilings or sides, for every person is ultimately a mystery. It doesn't send us up a one-way staircase. But it can give us a snapshot in time that will bring into the open our own new perspective-taking, the simple underpinnings of "Who We Are," and our developmental complexity.

The Three Questions

Discovering Who you are

To start on your development journey, we
only have to respond to three questions so
that we can discover and navigate these
healthy confusions. Our first course will help
you dive deeply into your present identity.
However, we know that we constantly
change, so we can continue to ask these
three questions throughout our lives to
discover who the "new me" is.

Developmental
Confussions

The second course in this series
"Developmental Confusions" will describe the
primary confusion at each development
stage that we must solve in order to continue
into our emerging identity.

Mind's I

12 Month Developmental Journey

The third course in this series "The
Mind's I" will lead us through the journey
of our life's path in practical ways. It
combines the past, present, and future
identities we navigate through and
confronts the healthy confusion we meet
and overcome at each stage.
